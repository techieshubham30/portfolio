How Social Media can help you find a job ?

As we know that each and everything relay on the internet and due to this convince we can look forward to a new era for job sector also. Nowadays recruiters find applicants' profile online on the social platforms to seetheire connections, technology enhancement, and their knowledge about advancing the future in technology. Social Media is playing a keen important role in finding jobs nowadays due to the pandemic the risky was a bit higher to find a job and social media gave a chance to every individual to find a job of their type. Some of the important advantages of using social media to find a job are that it increases job visibility as the applicant can apply according to their wishes it to shortens the hiring time as the applicant can upload his or her resume and if the job requirement is fulfilled the recruiter ask them to join. Using Social media as a networking tool can help every individual to grow their area of interest in different fields or it can help them to find unofficial job postings or find out about the new job opening through online connections at the companies you are interested in. Social media has become a way to interact with hundreds of people such as platforms like LinkedIn is the most suitable example.
