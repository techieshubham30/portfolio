How Social Media can help you find a job?

Published on July 28,2021

As we know that each and everything relays on internet and due to this convince we can look forward to a new era for job sector also . Now a days recruiters find applicants profile online on social platform to see there connections , technology enhancement and there knowledge about advancing future in technology . Social Media is playing a keen important role in finding job now a days due to pandemic the risky was bit higher to find a job and social media gave a chance to each and every individual to find a job of there type . Some of the important advantages of using social media to find a job is that it increases job visibility as the applicant can apply according to there wishes it to shortens the hiring time as the applicant can upload his or her resume and if it the job requirement is fulfilled the recruiter ask them to join .Using Social media as a networking tool can help each and every individual to grow there area of interest in different field or it can help them to find unofficial job postings or find out about new job opening through online connections at the companies you are interested in . Social media has become a way to interact with hundreds of people such as platforms like LinkedIn is the most suitable example .Some more websites which helped the individual to get jobs such as naukri , glass door act as a interface between applicant and recruiters . What makes social media soo much power full that we as applicants can know each and everything about company there working style , about there recruiting process etc . It shows your personality online rather then meeting in person . As social media is a part of most people's everyday lives but using it in an efficient manner is not known by all of them, linkedIn is the number one social media resource for job seekers- the site has a dedicated jobs section where as twitter is another resource as we can use hashtags to search for relevant job . Yes social media helps us to to indulge in all this but we have to remember managing our personal profile doesn't make you a social media expert . We all need to understand how companies are using social media and how it relates to industry and what value it can add to company.
